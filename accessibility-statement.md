This repo was built from muan.co and i want this site to be usable by everyone. Concretely, that means:

## What I do

- Every page is readable and navigable without JavaScript.
- Images carry meaningful `alt` text.
- Landmarks (`header`, `main`, `footer`, `nav`) are used properly, and every `nav` has an `aria-label`.
- Colour is never the only way information is conveyed.
- The site respects `prefers-color-scheme` and `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Text reflows and remains readable when zoomed.

## Known gaps

This section is honest rather than aspirational. If you find something broken, it belongs here — please tell me.

## Contact

If any part of this site is difficult for you to use, [open an issue](https://github.com/BadhriNadhBade/badhrinadh.com/issues) and I will fix it(hopefully).
