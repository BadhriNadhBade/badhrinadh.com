Posts live in `_posts/` and are named `YYYY-MM-DD-slug.md`. They appear on the home page only when the front matter includes `feature: 1`, which is how the home page stays short while the archive grows.

Setting `feature: 1` also enables the like button at the bottom of the page, via the OpenHeart protocol.

## Formatting

Markdown is parsed by CommonMark with `HARDBREAKS` on, so a single newline
becomes a line break — no need for two trailing spaces.

> Block quotes get a large decorative quote mark.

Inline `code` and fenced blocks both use the monospace stack.
